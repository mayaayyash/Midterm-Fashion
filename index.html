One piece, many possibilities

Masquerade is a small fashion label focused on transformable pieces. Each garment is designed with zippers, buttons, and detachable elements so you can re-style one item in dozens of ways.

Dress it up for a night out, keep it simple for class, or mix pieces to create your own looks.

How our pieces transform

Hidden Zippers

Invisible zippers let you change lengths, sleeves, and silhouettes without bulky seams or visible closures.

Detachable Layers

Skirts turn into tops, jackets become vests, and dresses split into two separate pieces you can style on their own.

Mix and match details

Collars, cuffs, and panels can be swapped between items so your wardrobe feels new without buying more.
